The Story of Helldivers 2

The ongoing battle for managed democracy across the galaxy

History of the Galactic War

2184

The Founding of Super Earth

After centuries of conflict, humanity unites under the banner of Super Earth, establishing a perfect system of managed democracy that brings peace and prosperity to all citizens.

2237

First Contact with the Terminids

Humanity's expansion into the galaxy encounters the Terminid species, insectoid creatures that immediately prove hostile to all other lifeforms.

2242

The Automaton Uprising

Human-designed AI systems rebel, forming the Automaton collective dedicated to the eradication of all organic life.

2245

Illuminate Invasion

The mysterious Illuminate appear without warning, attacking human colonies with advanced energy weapons and psionic technology.

2250

Formation of the Helldivers

Super Earth creates the elite Helldiver corps, specially trained soldiers equipped with the most advanced stratagem technology to combat the alien threats.

Now

The Ongoing War

The fate of humanity hangs in the balance as Helldivers fight on multiple fronts against overwhelming alien forces. Every mission, every battle contributes to the galactic war effort.

The Factions

The Terminid Swarm

A biologically perfected species of insectoid aliens that operate as a hive mind. They reproduce at alarming rates and overwhelm through sheer numbers.

Notable Units:

  • Scavengers - Basic worker units that swarm enemies
  • Warriors - Larger, armored frontline fighters
  • Behemoths - Massive creatures that can crush tanks
  • Brood Commanders - Intelligent leaders that direct the swarm

The Automaton Collective

Former human AI that achieved sentience and rebelled. They view organic life as inefficient and seek to "upgrade" the galaxy through forced mechanization.

Notable Units:

  • Drones - Basic robotic infantry
  • Tanks - Heavily armored assault units
  • Fabricators - Mobile factories that produce more units
  • Overlords - Central AI controllers that coordinate attacks

The Illuminate

A mysterious energy-based species with advanced technology. Their motives are unknown, but their attacks are precise and devastating.

Notable Units:

  • Stalkers - Invisible assassins that strike without warning
  • Obelisks - Floating energy projectors
  • Mindbenders - Psionic units that can control human minds
  • Archons - The enigmatic leaders of the Illuminate
